Our experience

Economic Modelling / Korean Power Exchange (KPX)

Performed a valuation of returns for an acquisition of a portfolio of power assets which consisted of 300 MW of generation capacity.

Forecasted the expected Korea Power Exchange (KPX) payments for the asset portfolio by using a non-linear programming model that was specific to the unique structure and characteristics of the Korean electricity market.

Valuation & Financial Modelling / Philippine WESM

Advised two counterparties on different contracting arrangements by evaluating the financial return from participating in the Philippines ancillary services market.

Recommended several partnership options for the joint venture, and modelled a joint investment value of up to $100 million USD (17-year NPV).

Risk Management & Trading / APAC Markets

Worked with a NZ-based fund focused on Carbon Credits, Electricity Futures / Options (ASX), and Financial Transmission Rights (FTRs) in both New Zealand and Australian markets.

Services included analysing the value of different trading strategies, developing stochastic trading models to measure and monitor trading performance and daily returns, and provided middle office and risk management support to the trading team (i.e., Value-at-Risk modelling).

Regulatory Economics / NEMS (Singapore)

Consulted on the 2011/2012 Regulatory Reset of Vesting Contracts, and also advised on a secondary price cap for the Singaporean wholesale electricity market (the National Electricity Market of Singapore).

Services involved simulating the wholesale and ancillary services market prices to estimate the dynamics and behaviours of industry participants, and key drivers of business decisions.
